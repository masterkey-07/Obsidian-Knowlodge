# Types of TDD 


- # In Out
	- ## Don't Know the Design
	- ## Know that the System is Closed
	- ## Start at the Center of the Project  

- # Out In 
	- ## Know the Design of the System
	- ## Know that the System is Complex
	- ## Start at the Border and build with Mocks
