**Conditional Probability**

$$P(A|B) = \frac{P(A) \cap P(B)}{P(B)}$$

If the events are independent

$$P(A|B) = P(A)$$

**Arrangements**

$$A_{n,p} = \frac{n!}{(n - p)!}$$

**Permutation**

$$A_{n,n} = \frac{n!}{(n - n)!} = n! = P_n$$

**Combination**

$$C_{n,p} = \binom{n}{p} = \frac{n!}{p!(n - p)!}$$
